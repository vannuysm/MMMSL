Welcome to the MMMSL

This site is intended primarily for players in the league and for league officials (referees). The site contains schedule, registration, roster, yellow/red card, game result, field, and league rule information.

This league is a recreational men's league with Open, Over-30, and Over-40 divisions. The league is a non-profit organization and is a member of Michigan Soccer Association (MSA) which is affiliated with the United States Soccer Federation (USSF). Greater Lansing Area Soccer Referee Association (GLASRA) assigns USSF certified referees to officiate all MMMSL sanctioned games.
